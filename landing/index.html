FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about VidaPulse.

What is VidaPulse?

VidaPulse is a video analytics platform for coaches, course creators, educators, and digital marketers. You paste any video URL — YouTube, Amazon S3, Google Drive, Dropbox, Loom, Zoom, or a direct MP4/HLS link — and VidaPulse gives you second-level engagement heatmaps, unique viewer tracking, play rate, average watch percentage, device and geographic data, UTM attribution, and a one-line embed script. No video re-hosting required.

Does VidaPulse require me to upload or re-host my video?

No. VidaPulse never stores your video files. Your video stays on YouTube, S3, Google Drive, Dropbox, or wherever it already lives. VidaPulse wraps it with an analytics player and collects engagement events. No migration, no storage costs, no lock-in.

What video platforms does VidaPulse support?

VidaPulse works with YouTube, Amazon S3, Google Drive, Azure Blob Storage, Dropbox, OneDrive, Loom, Zoom recordings, and any direct MP4, HLS, H.264, or MOV video URL.

Is VidaPulse free?

Yes. VidaPulse has a free forever plan — no trial period, no expiry, no credit card required. It supports 1 video with basic analytics. Paid plans start at $10/month (Starter, up to 10 videos) and $19/month (Pro, unlimited videos + heatmaps + viewer-level analytics).

What are engagement heatmaps?

Engagement heatmaps in VidaPulse show viewer attention second-by-second across your video's timeline. Each second is coloured green (high retention), yellow (moderate), or red (low). The heatmap tracks first-time watches separately from replays, so you can see which moments viewers return to. This helps you pinpoint drop-off points and optimise your content for higher retention. Available on the Pro plan.

How does the embed script work?

VidaPulse generates a single <script> tag. Paste it onto any page — a custom website, Kajabi, ClickFunnels, WordPress, Webflow, or plain HTML. The script automatically finds the video element, starts a viewer session, and sends play, pause, seek, rewatch, and exit events to VidaPulse. The tracker is under 5 KB, dependency-free, and works with native video, YouTube iFrame API, and Vimeo embeds.

How is VidaPulse different from YouTube Analytics?

YouTube Analytics only covers videos on YouTube and shows aggregated audience data without individual viewer sessions. VidaPulse works on any video source, tracks individual viewer sessions on your own website, captures UTM parameters and traffic sources, shows second-level heatmaps with first-watch vs replay breakdown, and stores data in your account — not locked inside YouTube's platform.

Who owns the viewer data collected by VidaPulse?

You do. All analytics data is stored in your VidaPulse account and is never sold or shared with third parties. The embed tracker identifies viewers by a UUID stored in localStorage — no personally identifiable information (PII) is collected and no cookies are set by the tracker script.
