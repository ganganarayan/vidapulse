Pricing & value

What does VidaPulse cost?

VidaPulse has three plans. The Free plan is free forever, with no card, and covers one video with basic stats. Starter is ten dollars a month and covers ten videos, adding geography, device, and average watch time. Pro is nineteen dollars a month and covers unlimited videos, adding a second-by-second heatmap, viewer-level history, segmentation, and conversion tracking. Every tier measures a video you already host anywhere, with no re-hosting and no personal data collected. The right tier depends on how many videos you have and how deep you need to see.

The Free plan

The Free plan is free forever and needs no card. It covers one video with basic stats, which is enough to read a real retention curve and the percentage of viewers reaching any point on your most important video.

It is meant to let you analyse the one video that matters most and feel the value before paying for anything. You paste a single video URL, embed one line of script or a script-free iframe, and there is no re-hosting. The second-by-second heatmap, viewer-level history, and conversion tracking are not on Free; those are Pro features. If you have one key video and only need to know roughly where viewers leave, Free may be all you need.

Starter, ten dollars a month

Starter is ten dollars a month and is the step up when one video is no longer enough. It covers ten videos and adds a few useful dimensions on top of the basics.

  • Ten videos, so a small portfolio of sales or product videos fits.
  • Geography, so you can see where your viewers are.
  • Device, so you can see how they watch, such as mobile versus desktop.
  • Average watch time, a clean summary of how long people stay.

Starter suits someone with several videos who wants more context than the basics but does not yet need the deepest precision features. It does not include the second-by-second heatmap, viewer-level history, or conversion tracking, which remain on Pro.

Pro, nineteen dollars a month

Pro is nineteen dollars a month and is the full picture. It covers unlimited videos and unlocks the deepest features.

  • Unlimited videos, so the whole library is covered.
  • Second-by-second heatmap, which pins the exact moment attention drops so you can tie it to a specific line or scene.
  • Viewer-level history, so you can see how individual viewers watched, including replays, with no personal data collected.
  • Segmentation, so you can compare how different sources or groups watch.
  • Conversion tracking, so you can tie watching to the outcome, such as a sale, booked call, or sign-up.

Pro suits someone who needs to know the precise moment of a drop, who watched what, and whether viewers who reached the offer actually converted.

Which tier fits you

Pick by two questions: how many videos do you need to track, and how deep do you need to see? One important video and a rough retention curve, Free. Several videos with geography, device, and watch time, Starter. Unlimited videos with a heatmap, viewer-level detail, and conversion tracking, Pro.

Hypothetical: suppose you run a single sales video and just want to know where people leave. The Free plan covers that. If you later add a product video and a demo and want to compare how mobile and desktop viewers watch, Starter at ten dollars a month fits. If you then need to pin a drop to the exact second and see whether finishers convert, Pro at nineteen dollars a month is the tier. Each step up answers a sharper question, so you only pay for the depth you actually reach.

How VidaPulse solves this

The simplest way to find your tier is to start on the Free plan, which is free forever for one video with no card. Paste your existing video URL from wherever it lives, embed one line of script or a script-free iframe, and there is no re-hosting. When you need more videos or more depth, move up: Starter at ten dollars a month for ten videos plus geography, device, and average watch time, or Pro at nineteen dollars a month for unlimited videos plus the second-by-second heatmap, viewer-level history, and conversion tracking.
