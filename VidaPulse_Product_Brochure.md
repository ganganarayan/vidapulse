## What Is VidaPulse?

VidaPulse is a video analytics platform for creators, marketers, and business owners who want to understand *how* their audience watches their videos — not just whether they clicked play.

Most video platforms (YouTube, Vimeo, Loom) tell you view counts. VidaPulse tells you **where viewers drop off, which seconds they re-watch, what percentage actually finish, and how engagement changes over time** — all from a single dashboard, regardless of where your videos are hosted.

You don't move your videos. You don't change your hosting. You simply add your video URL and VidaPulse starts tracking.

## Core Features

### 1 — Overview Dashboard
A single-screen summary of all your videos combined:
- **Total Views** — every time a page with your video embed loaded
- **Unique Views** — distinct visitors who landed on your video pages
- **Total Viewers** — sessions where someone actually pressed play
- **Unique Viewers** — distinct people who played your video
- **Total Watch Time** — cumulative hours watched across all videos
- **Top Videos** — your best performers ranked by engagement

### 2 — Per-Video Analytics
Click into any video for a full breakdown:
- **Avg. Watch %** — how far through the video your average viewer gets
- **Play Rate** — what % of page visitors actually press play
- **Completion Rate** — who watches to the very end
- **Drop-off Rate** — where exactly viewers leave and never come back
- **Watch Time** — total and average session watch time
- **Re-watches** — which viewers (and which moments) got rewatched
- **Engagement Heatmap** *(Pro)* — a second-by-second heat map showing which parts of your video hold attention and which lose it

### 3 — Audience Intelligence
Understand who is watching and from where:
- **Geography** — country and city-level breakdown
- **Devices** — desktop vs mobile vs tablet split
- **Browsers** — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and more

### 4 — Viewer Journey Funnel
A four-step funnel for every video showing where you lose your audience:
1. Landed on page
2. Started video (pressed play)
3. Reached 50%
4. Completed video

Drop-off percentages between each step are calculated automatically. No setup required.

### 5 — Live Events Log
A real-time stream of every analytics event across all your videos:
- Play, pause, seek, mute, unmute, ended
- Timestamped to the second
- Filterable by event type

### 6 — Alerts
Set rules and get notified without checking your dashboard every day:
- **Traffic Spike** — a video gets 3× its usual plays in 24 hours
- **Sudden Drop-off** — play rate falls below 30% for 3 days running
- **Viral Moment** — any video crosses 1,000 plays in under 24 hours
- **New Domain Embed** — your video appears on a website for the first time
- **Weekly Digest** — Monday morning recap of last week's top performers

Notifications delivered in-app and as browser push alerts.

### 7 — Reports
Export and schedule analytics reports:
- **Viewer Journey Export** — full CSV of every session (watch time, device, location, completion)
- Quarterly summaries
- Monthly performance snapshots

### 8 — Webhook Integration
Connect VidaPulse to your existing infrastructure:
- Send real-time analytics events to any server or automation tool
- Compatible with Make (Integromat), Zapier, and custom backends
- Secured with HMAC signature header (X-VP-Signature)

### 9 — Embed Code — One Click
Every video gets its own embed snippet. Copy and paste into any website, funnel page, or email.

## Privacy by Design

VidaPulse tracks **viewing behaviour**, never viewer identity.

- No names, emails, or phone numbers are collected from your audience
- Viewers are identified by an anonymous cookie UUID only
- Your audience's personal data stays entirely outside VidaPulse
- Your privacy policy with your own audience remains fully intact
- GDPR and CCPA compatible by architecture

This is a deliberate product decision — not a missing feature.
